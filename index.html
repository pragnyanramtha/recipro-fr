How the Tech works

Unlike Web2, where your data resides on vulnerable, centralized servers, ICP applications run entirely on a decentralized network. This eliminates the central points of control and data harvesting. Every interaction is on-chain, auditable, and immutable, yet designed to protect user privacy. This fundamental architectural difference ensures that your data truly belongs to you, empowering a future of genuine free speech, secure interactions, and unparalleled digital autonomy.
